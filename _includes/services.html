Mechanical

Mechanical Engineering is the part of Mechatronics Engineering that I am most experienced with. I love beautiful mechanical design and often I love to just think about the different mechanical problems as if it is a riddle or a puzzle. One of the most interesting designs for me are the gearboxes. I designed a CVT of my own version in high school and recently, I am fascinated by the harmonic drives and cycloidal drives for robotic applications. In my portfolio, many of the projects heavily involved solid mechanical design and as I learn more about robotics, I cannot wait to design beautiful robotic mechanisms. Lastly, one of the most amazing designs that I have come across is the load balancing system on the PR2 (Willow Garage) manipulator (The robot on the top of my website). If anyone wants to have a discussion about the PR2 design and teach me a thing or two, I am all yours!
